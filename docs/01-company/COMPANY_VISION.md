## 1. Vision

**Build India's leading AI-powered School Growth System.**

Every private school in India loses parents daily to unanswered WhatsApp messages,
missed calls, forgotten follow-ups, and no-show school visits. School Parent Enquiry AI
becomes the growth engine of these schools: an AI layer that answers every enquiry,
books every school visit, recovers every missed call, collects every review, and
re-engages every lapsed enquiry — across WhatsApp today, voice tomorrow.

The **AI WhatsApp Parent Enquiry System is Version 1** of this platform, not the company.

## 2. Mission

Ensure no Indian school ever loses a parent to a missed message. We give small and
mid-sized schools the front-office capability of a premier institution, at the price of
a part-time staff salary fraction.

## 3. Why now, why us

- WhatsApp is the default parent-communication channel in India; schools run their
  entire admissions funnel on it with zero automation.
- Meta Cloud API direct access, cheap capable models (GPT-5 nano class), and prompt
  caching make per-conversation cost near-zero — economics that didn't exist two years ago.
- We start narrow (K-12 school admissions) where enquiry volume is high during season,
  parent intent is high, and conversations are qualification-heavy — the perfect wedge
  vertical.
- Safeguarding-first architecture (never promises admission, never gives legal/custody
  advice, fail-closed handoff) is a durable trust moat in education, not a compliance
  checkbox.

## 4. Core Values

1. **Child safety over everything.** The AI never makes an admissions decision, and
   never touches a custody, disciplinary, or safeguarding matter itself. This is
   permanent and non-negotiable at every layer of the company.
2. **Schools grow, we grow.** Pricing, product, and metrics all align to one outcome:
   more qualified admission enquiries and booked school visits for the school.
3. **Trust through restraint.** The AI that says "let me check with our school office"
   earns more trust than one that guesses. We ship restraint deliberately.
4. **Small team, compounding systems.** Multi-tenant, no-code onboarding, and automation
   let a small team serve hundreds of schools.
5. **Truth in documentation.** Decisions are written down, versioned, and honoured.

## 6. Long-term positioning

School Parent Enquiry AI is not a chatbot vendor, not a WhatsApp marketing tool, and not
a school ERP/SIS. We are the **enquiry-side operating layer** of the school: everything
between "a parent tried to reach the school" and "the parent sat down for an admission
counseling visit" is ours. ERP/SIS systems own the academic and administrative record; we
own the enquiry funnel — and integrate with them rather than replace them.
